Shipping Information

1. Delivery Times

Standard shipping within Switzerland: 2-3 business days
International shipping: 5-10 business days

2. Shipping Costs

Switzerland: Free shipping on orders over 50 CHF
International: Calculated at checkout based on destination

3. Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information.

4. International Orders

Please note that import duties and taxes are not included in the product price or shipping cost. These charges are the buyer's responsibility.
