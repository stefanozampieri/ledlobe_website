About LEDLOBE

Perfect for festivals, parties and events. It can be your accessory for the party you have next week or a present for your niece's birthday. Try the five different colors available. Follow us on instagram @ledlobe_official.

User Manual

FAQ

How long does the battery lasts?

The battery lasts 12h. Then you can replace the battery. Use a standard CR1025 battery.

Is it safe for the skin?

Yes. The back of the earring is coated with skin safe silicon and the earring post is gold plated to ensure a good contact with the skin.

Where can you ship?

The standard shipping if you order from the online shop is to Switzerland, Italy and France. Please send us an e-mail for shipping to specific countries.
