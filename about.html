About LedLobe

How Two Friends Turned an Idea Into Light

The concept behind LedLobe was born in Switzerland when two engineers were getting ready for a festival and asked themselves a simple question: What if the crowd became a part of the show?

We then spent hours brainstorming how to create something unique and creative—something that would make everyone shine. That’s when we came up with the concept that started it all: LED earrings that bring light, energy, and personality to any party you attend.

Today, LedLobe continues to embody that same spirit of innovation and fun. Whether in small, intimate gatherings or large-scale events, our earrings transform spaces into unforgettable experiences—all born from one idea and two friends with a passion to make the light glow.

User Manual

FAQ

How long does the battery lasts?

The battery lasts 12h. Then you can replace the battery. Use a standard CR1025 battery.

Is it safe for the skin?

Yes. The back of the earring is coated with skin safe silicon and the earring post is gold plated to ensure a good contact with the skin.

Where can you ship?

The standard shipping if you order from the online shop is to Switzerland, Italy and France. Please send us an e-mail for shipping to specific countries.
